The Problem

  • Students struggle to take complete notes during fast lectures
  • Important concepts and definitions get missed
  • No immediate way to test understanding after class
  • Teachers can't identify which topics students find difficult
  • Traditional recording playback is time-consuming

Our Solution

Upload a lecture recording or paste a YouTube link — AI does the rest.

Transcription

Whisper converts speech to text with timestamps

Smart Notes

Gemini AI generates structured study notes

Key Concepts

NLP extracts important terms and definitions

Voice Analysis

Librosa detects teacher emphasis patterns

Quiz Generation

AI creates MCQ questions from lecture content

Teacher Analytics

Dashboard shows class-wide weak/strong topics

NLP Concept Extraction

How we extract meaningful keywords from lecture transcripts:

Step 1 — Tokenization

Break transcript into individual words using regex

Step 2 — Filtering

Remove stopwords (a, the, is), noise words (guess, listen), and detected names

Step 3 — Scoring

Rank by: word frequency × emphasis boost (if teacher emphasized that segment)

Step 4 — Definitions

Pattern match phrases like "X is defined as", "X means", "X refers to"
